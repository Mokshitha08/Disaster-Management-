Process

From Crisis to Recovery

The four-phase approach aligned with the Fast Restart methodology from the IEEE research paper.

01

Assess & Identify

Evaluate personnel availability, supply chain status, and financial position. Apply Stoplight ratings to all critical items.

02

Plan & Assign

Create detailed recovery plans, assign team leaders, define recovery steps, and set target timelines for each department.

03

Execute & Monitor

Track plan execution, update risk statuses in real time, and monitor cash flow and supply chain reconstruction progress.

04

Review & Improve

Document lessons learned, archive completed plans, and update the organisation's disaster preparedness for the next event.

Key Findings Applied

Stoplight Approach

Green/Yellow/Red ratings for supply chain items, processes, and customer interactions to identify at-risk elements immediately.

Fast Restart Protocol

"Plans are worthless, planning is everything." — Eisenhower. Move forward rapidly with a structured restart methodology.

Cash Flow Priority

80% of profitable small businesses that file for bankruptcy do so due to lack of cash flow — monitor continuously until positive.
